Frequently Asked Question's

Why you want to choose CodeBoosters?

We are the leading virtual internship providers in the industry and by gaining experiences from our internship program you'll get the opportunity to land on the high paid industry job. Our Internship program is designed for both beginners and advance level developers.

How to apply for CodeBoosters Virtual Internship Program ?

You need to fill the interest form on your domain of interest and also apply from AICTE Internship portal when we get interns from there.

What is duration of the internship ?

CBVIP - CodeBoosters Virtual Internship Program is a 1 Month Program.

When i get the confirmation after applying?

You'll get the confirmation from our side before one week of your tenure period starts.

Is this Internship free ?

Yes, CBVIP is completely free internship program but you need to pay the documentation fees Rs.249/- for the certification.

What is the purpose of documentation ?

It's completely for the documentation expenses and you'll get the certificate of completion with the lifetime verification support.
